Events

Omaha Summer Arts Festival

Date: Mid-June (annually)

Location: Aksarben Village or downtown Omaha

Description: A vibrant celebration featuring local and national artists, live music, food vendors, and family-friendly activities. Free and open to the public.

Jazz on the Green

Date: Thursdays, mid-July to mid-August

Location: Turner Park at Midtown Crossing

Description: Free summer jazz concert series with performances by local and national artists. Open seating, bring blankets or chairs.

Nebraska State Fair

Date: Late August to early September

Location: Grand Island, NE (widely promoted in Omaha)

Description: A large regional fair with rides, concerts, exhibits, food, and entertainment. Many Omaha residents and businesses participate.
